WellCOM

A smart connected device to controle and regulate water pumping
in resource limited environements.

WellCOM is an eco-friendly, economic and user-friendly system that can be easily installed on existing solar water pumps. It is meant to ease the access to solar water pumps for Burkinabé families as it is based on a credit system. Farmers can thus only pay for the water they use thanks to a user-friendly Telegram bot to credit their device. It also provides a functional dashboard and telegram group for the company to gather information about active devices and the power plant efficiency via several sensors.
